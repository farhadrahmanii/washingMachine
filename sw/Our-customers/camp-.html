Kamisheni ya Kifalme ya Saudi Arabia Ras Al-Khair Labour Camp

Nyumba>Wateja wetu

Ras Al-Khair (also known as Ras Az-Zour, Ras Azzour) is a town and a port (currently under development) on the eastern coast of Saudi Arabia, 60 km from north of Jubail. It is also known under its project name "Minerals Industrial City".

“Ras Al-Khair Labor Camp is a big project and there are many workers, so, it has a lot of laundry work to do every day. We didn’t know much about the industrial laundry equipment but Washun helped us with all the details including the equipment analysis, laundry room layout design, installation etc. After the machines were installed, the machines worked very well. We are really happy with these machines.”
