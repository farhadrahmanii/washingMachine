Washun meets three different kind of requirements for hotels. Firstly, the laundry machines are long-time operated (durability). Secondly, they can wash and dry a lot of laundry in a cycle (efficiency). Thirdly, they can wash the laundry very clean (cleanliness). Washun has a strict Quality Control and Testing System, good spare parts suppliers, such as Japanese NSK bearing, LS inverter, Siemens chip etc., to make sure our machines’ quality is guaranteed.

Hotels always have a lot of laundry to be washed every day, including bed sheets, quilt covers, towels, pillow cases, workers’ uniforms etc. So, the laundry washing’s cleanliness, efficiency and durability are very important. This is why our machines have a very good reputation in the market. Washun has supplied more than 1000 hotels in local market and more than 1600 hotels  overseas. Successful hotels we have cooperated with are Hilton Hotel, Shangri-La Hotel, Vienna Hotel etc.
